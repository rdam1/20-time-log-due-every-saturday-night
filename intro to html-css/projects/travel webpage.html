Agrihan (also spelled Agrigan) is an island in the Northern Mariana Islands in the Pacific Ocean. The island is currently uninhabited. Agrihan is located 62 kilometers (39 mi) to the north of Pagan. Source: Wikipedia

Additional Info

Did you know that:

  • From a European perspective Agrihan was discovered on June 11, 1522
  • The entire island is a massive stratovolcano which rises over 4,000 meters (13,120 ft) from the ocean floor, and is the fifth largest in the Marianas volcanic arc
  • Agrihan has an approximate area of 44 km^2
