Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time:
    • Prep: Cook: Ready in:
    20 mins 10 mins 1 hour
  • Servings: 24
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flower 3 cups
baking soda 1 teaspoon
butter, softened 1 cup
chopped walnuts 1 cup
eggs 2
hot water 2 teaspoons
packed brown sugar 1 cup
salt 1/2 teaspoon
semisweet chocolate chips 2 cups
vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
white sugar 1 cup
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Brownies

  • Time:
    • Prep: Cook: Ready in:
    25 mins 35 mins 1 hour
  • Servings: 16
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flower 1/2 cup
baking powder 1/4 teaspoon
butter 1 cup
eggs 2
salt 1/4 teaspoon
unsweetened cocoa powder 1/3 cup
vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
white sugar 1 cup
Frosting:
Ingredients Quantity
butter, softened 3 tablespoons
confectioner's sugar 3 tablespoons
honey 1 tablespoon
unsweetened cocoa powder 3 tablespoons
vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
  4. To Make Frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.

Red Velvet Cupcakes

  • Time:
    • Prep: Cook: Ready in:
    30 mins 20 mins 50 mins
  • Servings: 20
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flower 2 cups
baking soda 1 1/2 teaspoons
butter 1/2 cup
buttermilk 1 cup
distilled white vinegar 1 tablespoon
eggs 2
red food colouring 1 ounce
salt 1 teaspoon
unsweetened cocoa powder 1/3 cup
vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
white sugar 1 1/2 cups
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two 12 cup muffin pans or line with 20 paper baking cups.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Mix in the eggs, buttermilk, red food coloring and vanilla. Stir in the baking soda and vinegar. Combine the flour, cocoa powder and salt; stir into the batter just until blended. Spoon the batter into the prepared cups, dividing evenly.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the tops spring back when lightly pressed, 20 to 25 minutes. Cool in the pan set over a wire rack. When cool, arrange the cupcakes on a serving platter and frost with desired frosting.
